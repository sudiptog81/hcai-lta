

Supreme Court of India

Equivalent citations: 1998 232 ITR 359 SC, (2000) 10 SCC 501

Author: S C Sen

Bench: S Sen, S Quadri

ORDER Suhas Chandra Sen, J.

1\. We have perused the order of the High Court. We have also seen the
agreement. We are not persuaded to hold that the view taken by the High Court
is erroneous ; the appeals are dismissed. There will be no order as to costs.
