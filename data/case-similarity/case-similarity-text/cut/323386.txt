Central Government Act
Section 209 in The Indian Penal Code
209\. Dishonestly making false claim in Court.—Whoever fraudu­lently or
dishonestly, or with intent to injure or annoy any person, makes in a Court of
Justice any claim which he knows to be false, shall be punished with
imprisonment of either descrip­tion for a term which may extend to two years,
and shall also be liable to fine.
