31 Provisions as to barred or pending suits, etc:- Nothing in this Act shall,—
(a) enable any suit, appeal or application to be instituted, preferred or
made, for which the period of limitation prescribed by the Indian Limitation
Act, 1908 (9 of 1908), expired before the commencement of this Act; or
(b) affect any suit, appeal or application instituted, preferred or made
before, and pending at, such commencement.
