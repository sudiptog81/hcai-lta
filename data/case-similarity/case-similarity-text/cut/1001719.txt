Central Government Act
Article 5(1) in The Constitution Of India 1949
(1) Notwithstanding anything in this Constitution, the Governor may by public
notification direct that any particular Act of Parliament or of the
Legislature of the State shall not apply to a Scheduled Area or any part
thereof in the State or shall apply to a Scheduled Area or any part thereof in
the State subject to such exceptions and modifications as he may specify in
the notification and any direction given under this sub paragraph may be given
so as to have retrospective effect
