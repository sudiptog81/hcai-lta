Central Government Act
Section 9 in The Indian Contract Act, 1872
9\. Promises, express and implied.—In so far as the proposal or acceptance of
any promise is made in words, the promise is said to be express. In so far as
such proposal or acceptance is made otherwise than in words, the promise is
said to be implied. —In so far as the proposal or acceptance of any promise is
made in words, the promise is said to be express. In so far as such proposal
or acceptance is made otherwise than in words, the promise is said to be
implied."
