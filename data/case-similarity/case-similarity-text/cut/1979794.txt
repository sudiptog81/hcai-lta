41\. Keeping in view the expression "cause of action" used in clause (2) of
Article 226 of the Constitution of India, indisputably even if a small
fraction thereof accrues within the jurisdiction of the Court, the Court will
have jurisdiction in the matter though the doctrine of forum conveniens may
also have to be considered.
