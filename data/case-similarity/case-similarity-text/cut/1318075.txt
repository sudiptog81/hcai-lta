Central Government Act
Section 145(3) in The Income- Tax Act, 1995
(3) Where the Assessing Officer is not satisfied about the correctness or
completeness of the accounts of the assessee, or where the method of
accounting provided in sub- section (1) or accounting standards as notified
under sub- section (2), have not been regularly followed by the assessee, the
Assessing Officer may make an assessment in the manner provided in section
144."
Reopening of assessment at the instance of the assessee.
