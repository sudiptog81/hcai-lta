39. Regulations.-
(1) The authorities of the University may make Regulations consistent with the
Act, the Statutes and the Ordinances for the following matters, namely:--
(i) laying down the procedure to be observed at their meetings and the number
of members required to form a quorum;
(ii) providing for all matters which are required by the Act, the Statutes or
the Ordinance to be prescribed by Regulations;
(iii) providing for all other matters solely concerning such authorities or
committees appointed by them and not provided for by the Act, the Statutes or
the Ordinances.
(2) Every authority of the University shall make Regulations providing for the
giving of notice to the members of such authority of the dates of meeting and
