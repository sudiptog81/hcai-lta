The result of the aforesaid discussion is that Criminal Appeal No.497 of 2001
is allowed, the impugned judgment of the High Court is set aside. The trial
court is directed to proceed from the stage of framing of charge. Having
regard to the fact that the charges were framed about 11 years ago we direct
the trial court to expeditiously conclude the trial and as far as possible it
shall be held from day-to-day.
