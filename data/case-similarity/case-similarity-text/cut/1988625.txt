33\. Even in a case of title, Explanation IV to Section 11 would apply. (See
also Sulochana Amma vs. Narayanan Nair : 1994 (2) SCC 14).
34\. Furthermore in terms of Section 5 of the Specific Relief Act, 1963 a suit
for possession must be filed having regard to the provisions of the Code of
Civil Procedure. If the statute provides for the applicability of  23 the Code
of Civil Procedure, there cannot be any doubt whatsoever that all the relevant
provisions thereof shall apply. (See Shamsu Suhara Beevi vs. G. Alex and
another : (2004) 8 SCC 569) & Hardesh Ores (P) Ltd. vs. Hede and Company :2007
(5) SCC 614).
35\. We have, therefore, no hesitation to hold that the impugned judgment
cannot be sustained. The same is set aside. The appeal is allowed with costs.
Counsel's fee assessed at Rs.25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand only).
