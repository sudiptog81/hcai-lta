28\. We are of the considered opinion that the caustic remarks made by the
High Court, against the government pleaders and the Court staff clearly
exhibits a departure from the principles quoted above.
29\. We are of the considered opinion that the judgment of the High Court is
unsustainable either in law or in equity. Consequently, the appeals are
allowed. The impugned judgment of the High Court is set aside with no order as
to costs.
