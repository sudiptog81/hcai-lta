there is now no live link between the alleged prejudicial activities and the
purpose of detention now. He has also relied upon the decisions of this Court
in T.A. Abdul Rehman vs. State of Kerala and others AIR 1990 SC 225 State of

50\. In our opinion, it is not necessary to go into this submission of Shri
Soli Sorabjee since we are of the opinion that the petition deserves to be
allowed on the first ground, namely, that the relevant material was not placed
before the Detaining Authority, and this vitiates the detention order.
