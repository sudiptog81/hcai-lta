Central Government Act
Section 536(2) in The Companies Act, 1956
(2) In the case of a winding up by or subject to the supervision of the Court,
any disposition of the property (including actionable claims) of the company,
and any transfer of shares in the company or alteration in the status of its
members, made after the commencement of the winding up, shall, unless the
Court otherwise orders, be void.
