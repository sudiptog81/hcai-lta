328\. Causing hurt by means of poison, etc., with intent to commit an
offence.â€”Whoever administers to or causes to be taken by any person any poison
or any stupefying, intoxicating or unwholesome drug, or other thing with
intent to cause hurt to such person, or with intent to commit or to facilitate
the commission of an offence or knowing it to be likely that he will thereby
