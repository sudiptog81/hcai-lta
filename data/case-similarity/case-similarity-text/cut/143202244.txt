269\. With the greatest of respect, Counsel appearing in support of the
appeals have made general arguments with regard to the differences between
provisions of the Central Excise Act or the Customs Act with the NDPS Act.
However, they have not specifically shown how exactly the powers of NDPS
officers conducting an investigation of an offence under the NDPS Act are
different from those of the Central Excise Officers, Customs officers and/or
Railway Protection Force Officers conducting an inquiry into an offence under
the provisions of those Acts.
270\. As observed above, the provisions of the Cr.P.C do not apply to an
inquiry/investigation under the NDPS Act except to the limited extent provided
in Section 50(5) and 51. Section 173 of the Cr.P.C has not been made
applicable to the NDPS Act.
