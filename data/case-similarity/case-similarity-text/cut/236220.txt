Central Government Act
Section 8(1) in The Patents Act, 1970
(1) Where an applicant for a patent under this Act is prosecuting either alone
or jointly with any other person an application for a patent in any country
outside India in respect of the same or substantially the same invention, or
where to his knowledge such an application is being prosecuted by some person
through whom he claims or by some person deriving title from him, he shall
file along with his application 30 [or subsequently 31 [within the prescribed
period as the Controller may allow]]-
32 [(a) a statement setting out detailed particulars of such application; and]
(b) an undertaking that, 33 [upto the date of grant of patent in India], he
would keep the Controller informed in writing, from time to time, of 34
[detailed particulars as required under] clause (a) in respect of every other
application relating to the same or substantially the same invention, if any,
filed in any country outside India subsequently to the filing of the statement
referred to in the aforesaid clause, within the prescribed time.
