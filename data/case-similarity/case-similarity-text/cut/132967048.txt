(2) The police shall investigate the complaint in accordance with the general

also be filed by the Appropriate Government or the Appropriate Commission or
any of their officer authorized by them or a Chief Electrical Inspector or an
Electrical Inspector or an authorized officer of Licensee or a generating
company, as the case may be directly in the appropriate court.
