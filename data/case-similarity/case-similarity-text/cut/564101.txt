Central Government Act
Section 48 in The Patents Act, 1970
116 [ 117 [ 48 Rights of patentees. -Subject to the other provisions contained
in this Act and the conditions specified in section 47, a patent granted under
this Act shall confer upon the patentee-
(a) where the subject matter of the patent is a product, the exclusive right
to prevent third parties, who do not have his consent, from the act of making,
using, offering for sale, selling or importing for those purposes that product
in India;
(b) where the subject matter of the patent is a process, the exclusive right
to prevent third parties, who do not have his consent, from the act of using
that process, and from the act of using, offering for sale, selling or
importing for those purposes the product obtained directly by that process in
India:
