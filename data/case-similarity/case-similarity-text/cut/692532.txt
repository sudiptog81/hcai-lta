Central Government Act
Section 87 in The Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881
87\. Effect of material alteration.—Any material alteration of a negotiable
instrument renders the same void as against any one who is a party thereto at
the time of making such alteration and does not consent thereto, unless it was
made in order to carry out the common intention of the original parties;
Alteration by indorsee.—And any such alteration, if made by an indorsee,
discharges his indorser from all liability to him in respect of the
consideration thereof. The provisions of this section are subject to those of
sections 20, 49, 86 and 125.
