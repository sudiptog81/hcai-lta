Central Government Act
Article 12(2) in The Constitution Of India 1949
(2) The person who was holding office immediately before the commencement of
this Constitution as Auditor General of India and has become on such
commencement the Comptroller and Auditor General of India under article 377
shall in addition to the salary specified in sub paragraph ( 1 ) of this
paragraph be entitled to receive as special pay an amount equivalent to the
difference between the salary so specified and the salary which he was drawing
as Auditor General of India immediately before such commencement
