There is another aspect of this matter which cannot be overlooked. Appellant
No.1 made a confession. He felt repentant not only while making the
confessional statement before the Judicial Magistrate, but also before the
learned Sessions Judge in his statement under Section 313 of the Code of
Criminal Procedure.
It is, therefore, in our opinion, not a case where extreme death penalty
should be imposed. We, therefore, are of the opinion that imposition of
punishment of rigorous imprisonment for life shall meet the ends of justice.
