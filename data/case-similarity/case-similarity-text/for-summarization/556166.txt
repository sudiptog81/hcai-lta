Central Government Act
Section 468 in The Indian Penal Code
468\. Forgery for purpose of cheating.—Whoever commits forgery, intending that
the 1[document or electronic record forged] shall be used for the purpose of
cheating, shall be punished with imprisonment of either de­scription for a
term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
