Central Government Act
Section 3 in The Research and Development Cess Act, 1986
3\. Levy and collection of cess on payments made towards import of
technology.—
(1) There shall be levied and collected, for the purposes of this Act, a cess
at such rate not exceeding five per cent. on all payments made towards the
import of technology, as the Central Government may, from time to time,
specify, by notification, in the Official Gazette.
(2) The cess shall be payable to the Central Government by an industrial
concern which imports technology on or before making any payments towards such
import and shall be paid by the industrial concern to any specified agency.
