Central Government Act
Section 22 in The Limitation Act, 1963
22\. Continuing breaches and torts.—In the case of a continuing breach of
contract or in the case of a continuing tort, a fresh period of limitation
begins to run at every moment of the time during which the breach or the tort,
as the case may be, continues. 22. Continuing breaches and torts.—In the case
of a continuing breach of contract or in the case of a continuing tort, a
fresh period of limitation begins to run at every moment of the time during
which the breach or the tort, as the case may be, continues."
