Central Government Act
Section 68A in the Copyright Act, 1957
1[68A. Penalty for contravention of section 52A.—Any person who publishes a
2[sound recording] or a video film in contravention of the provisions of
section 52A shall be punishable with imprisonment which may extend to three
years and shall also be liable to fine.]
