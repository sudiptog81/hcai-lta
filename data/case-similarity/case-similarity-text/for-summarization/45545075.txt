"Having regard to the submissions made by the learned counsels for the
parties, and to the impugned order passed by the trial court, it clearly
transpires that the respondent-plaintiff had established prima facie before
the trial court that their trademark "Maheshari" as shown in the image
registered in the registration certificate was being used since 2003 and that
the said certificate was valid upto May 2013. From the original labels
produced by the learned counsel for the respondent for the perusal of the
Court, it transpires that not only the word "Manga leshwari" used by the
appellant is phonetically and visually similar to the word "Maheshari", but
