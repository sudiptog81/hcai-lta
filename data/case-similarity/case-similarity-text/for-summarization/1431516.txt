Central Government Act
Section 27 in The Indian Contract Act, 1872
27\. Agreement in restraint of trade, void.—Every agreement by which any one
is restrained from exercising a lawful profession, trade or business of any
kind, is to that extent void. —Every agreement by which any one is restrained
from exercising a lawful profession, trade or business of any kind, is to that
extent void." Exception 1.—Saving of agreement not to carry on business of
which goodwill is sold.—One who sells the goodwill of a business may agree
with the buyer to refrain from carrying on a similar business, within
specified local limits, so long as the buyer, or any person deriving title to
the goodwill from him, carries on a like business therein, provided that such
limits appear to the Court reasonable, regard being had to the nature of the
business. 16 [***]
