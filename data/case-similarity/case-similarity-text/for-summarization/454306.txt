Central Government Act
Section 30 in The Income- Tax Act, 1995
30\. Rent, rates, taxes, repairs and insurance for buildings In respect of
rent, rates, taxes, repairs and insurance for premises, used for the purposes
of the business or profession, the following deductions shall be allowed-
(a) where the premises are occupied by the assessee-
(i) as a tenant, the rent paid for such premises; and further if he has
undertaken to bear the cost of repairs to the premises, the amount paid on
account of such repairs;
(ii) otherwise than as a tenant, the amount paid by him on account of current
repairs to the premises;
(b) any sums paid on account of land revenue, local rates or municipal taxes;
(c) the amount of any premium paid in respect of insurance against risk of
damage or destruction of the premises.
