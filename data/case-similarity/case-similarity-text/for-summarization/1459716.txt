Central Government Act
Section 4 in The Indian Partnership Act, 1932
4\. Definition of “partnership”, “partner”, “firm” and “firm
name”.—’’Partnership” is the relation between persons who have agreed to share
the profits of a business carried on by all or any of them acting for all.
Persons who have entered into partnership with one another are called
individually “partners” and collectively a “firm”, and the name under which
their business is carried on is called the “firm name”.
