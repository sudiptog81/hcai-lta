Central Government Act
Section 69A in The Income- Tax Act, 1995
69A. 3 Unexplained money, etc. Where in any financial year the assessee is
found to be the owner of any money, bullion, jewellery or other valuable
article and such money, bullion, jewellery or valuable article is not recorded
in the books of account, if any, maintained by him for any source of income,
and the assessee offers no explanation about the nature and source of
acquisition of the money, bullion, jewellery or other valuable article, or the
explanation offered by him is not, in the opinion of the 4 Assessing] Officer,
satisfactory, the money and the value of the bullion, jewellery or other
valuable article may be deemed to be the income of the assessee for such
financial year.]
