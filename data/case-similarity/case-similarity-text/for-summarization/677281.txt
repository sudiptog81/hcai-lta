Central Government Act
Section 31 in The Income- Tax Act, 1995
31\. Repairs and insurance of machinery, plant and furniture In respect of
repairs and insurance of machinery, plant or furniture used for the purposes
of the business or profession, the following deductions shall be allowed-
(i) the amount paid on account of current repairs thereto;
(ii) the amount of any premium paid in respect of insurance against risk of
damage or destruction thereof.
