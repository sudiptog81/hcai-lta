Central Government Act
Section 34 in the Central Excise Act, 1944
34\. Option to pay fine in lieu of confiscation.—Wherever confis­cation is
adjudged under this Act or the rules made thereunder, the officer adjudging it
shall give the owner of the goods an option to pay in lieu of confiscation
such fine as the officer thinks fit.
