Central Government Act
Section 64 in the Copyright Act, 1957
64\. Power of police to seize infringing copies.—1[
(1) Any police officer, not below the rank of a sub-inspector, may, if he is
satisfied that an offence under section 63 in respect of the infringement of
copyright in any work has been, is being, or is likely to be, committed, seize
without warrant, all copies of the work, and all plates used for the purpose
of making infringing copies of the work, wherever found, and all copies and
plates so seized shall, as soon as practicable, be produced before a
Magistrate.]—5[(1) Any police officer, not below the rank of a sub-inspector,

all copies and plates so seized shall, as soon as practicable, be produced
