Central Government Act
Section 10(2) in the Copyright Act, 1957
(2) A Deputy Registrar of Copyrights shall discharge under the superintendence
and direction of the Registrar of Copyrights such functions of the Registrar
under this Act as the Registrar may, from time to time, assign to him; and any
reference in this Act to the Registrar of Copyrights shall include a reference
to a Deputy Registrar of Copyrights when so discharging any such functions.
