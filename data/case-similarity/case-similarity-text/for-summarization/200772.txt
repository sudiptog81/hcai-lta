Central Government Act
Section 85 in The Indian Evidence Act, 1872
85\. Presumption as to powers-of-attorney.—The Court shall presume that every
document purporting to be a power-of-attorney, and to have been executed
before, and authenticated by, a Notary Public, or any Court, Judge,
Magistrate, 1[Indian] Consul or Vice-Consul, or representative 2[***] of the
3[Central Government], was so executed and authenticated.
