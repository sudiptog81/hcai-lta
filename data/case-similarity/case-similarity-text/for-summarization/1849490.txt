Central Government Act
Section 7 in The Income- Tax Act, 1995
7\. Income deemed to be received The following incomes shall be deemed to be
received in the previous year:-
(i) the annual accretion in the previous year to the balance at the credit of
an employee participating in a recognised provident fund, to the extent
provided in rule 6 of Part A of the Fourth Schedule; (ii) the transferred
balance in a recognised provident fund, to the extent provided in sub- rule
(4) of rule 11 of Part A of the Fourth Schedule.
